Human Factor

In this page, the human factors of the perpetrators and the victims will be analyzed. From the result, we will figure out what kind of factors of human would have the higher potential to become a perpetrator.





The age distribution of the killer and victim

We can see the peak of the killer is 20 years old, and for the victim, the peak is at 25 years old. Furthermore, there is a peack at 1 years old for victim, this may cause from domestic violence.




The race distribution of the killer

From this pie chart, it shows that the most killers are black and the second are white.

Furthermore, for the high Unknown part, I think it might because some perpetrators were not been caught yet.




The race distribution of the victim

From this pie chart, it shows that the most victim are black , it even takes more than 50% .
